ALL ABOUT US

Taquitos was born in Mexico in 2005. Through years of priding ourselves on freshness and quality, our authentic Mexico City-style taqueria has grown steadily since, with more than 25 locations in 4 countries. Keep an eye out for our first location.

Taquitos is located in the Prospect Heights neighborhood where our Chef is cooking innovative Mexican cuisine. The Spanish translation of “Taquitos” is synonym “high quality” and it’s our goal to provide that with every dish that we serve.
